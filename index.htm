Unity Demos

CAUTION: The demos are not tested on mobile devices which don't have a keyboard. It is advised to play the demos on a device which have a keyboard.

  • Helicopter (Basic input handling, object translation and rotation)
    • A helicopter and a demo landscape. Nothing else.
    • Control: WASD or arrow keys.
  • PBS & lighting scene 1 (Scene building)
    • Control: WASD or arrow keys.
  • PBS & lighting scene 2 (Skybox)
    • Control: WASD or arrow keys.
  • PBS & lighting scene 3 (Lighting)
    • Control: WASD or arrow keys.
  • Cannon (Physics & audio part 1)
    • Hit the target.
    • Control: Up and down arrow keys for the cannon angle, hold the space bar to charge and shoot.
  • Campfire + FPS controller (Physics & audio part 2)
    • Control: WASD or arrow keys.
    • Warning: Since no walls are defined around the map, be careful of falling out of the map.
  • Treasure Box (Animations part 1a)
    • Walk near the box to open it.
    • Control: WASD or arrow keys.
  • Coins (Animations part 1b)
    • Collect the coins.
    • Control: WASD or arrow keys.
  • Crystal 1 (UI & game logic)
    • Collect as many boxes as you can within the time limit.
    • Control: WASD or arrow keys, left shift for running.
    • Warning: Since no walls are defined around the map, be careful of falling out of the map.
  • Crystal 2 (Dialogues)
    • Control: WASD or arrow keys, left shift for running.
    • Warning: Since no walls are defined around the map, be careful of falling out of the map.
  • Fountain (Particle effects)
    • Four water taps.
    • Control: WASD or arrow keys.
